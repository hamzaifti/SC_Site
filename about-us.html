About Us
Sultan Concrete: Excellence in Pavers and Precast Concrete

Sultan Concrete is a leading company specializing in high-quality pavers and precast concrete products, dedicated to delivering durable, aesthetically pleasing solutions for diverse construction and landscaping needs. We leverage state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities and a skilled workforce to ensure superior quality and performance.

As part of the prestigious Crystalline Group of Companies, Sultan Concrete benefits from a rich heritage of industry expertise. The Crystalline Group, established in 1989, began as a sugar mill and has since expanded into a distinguished conglomerate with major contributions in Extra Neutral Alcohol, Nutraceuticals, and Construction. Known as the largest exporter of Extra Neutral Alcohol from Pakistan, Crystalline Group has built an international reputation for excellence.

Our Sister Companies Include:

  • Neuvic Pharma: Renowned for its advancements in the healthcare sector.
  • Crystalline Chemicals Pvt Ltd: Excels in chemical manufacturing and supply.
  • Horizon Chemicals: A distribution hub for Eastern Seas Holding Co. K.S.C, a leading industrial group from Kuwait. Horizon Chemicals specializes in high-quality, reasonably priced industrial chemicals and offers unrivaled handling expertise for its customers.
